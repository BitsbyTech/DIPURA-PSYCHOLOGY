Frequently Asked Questions

What can I expect in the first session?

In your first session, we'll focus on understanding your reasons for seeking therapy and discuss your goals. It's an opportunity for us to get to know each other and establish a comfortable and confidential environment where you can share openly.

How confidential are the sessions?

Confidentiality is a cornerstone of my practice. All information shared during sessions is kept strictly confidential, except in cases where disclosure is required by law, such as imminent risk of harm to yourself or others.

Do you accept insurance?

Currently, I do not accept insurance. However, I can provide you with a receipt or invoice that you may submit to your insurance provider for possible reimbursement, depending on your coverage plan.

What is your cancellation policy?

If you need to cancel or reschedule an appointment, please provide at least 24 hours' notice. Late cancellations or missed appointments may incur a fee equal to the session rate.

How do I know which therapy is right for me?

During our initial consultation, we'll discuss your specific needs and concerns. Based on this, I'll recommend the most suitable therapy approach to support your journey toward well-being.

Can I schedule a consultation before committing to therapy?

Yes, I offer an initial 15-minute phone consultation at no charge. This allows us to determine if we're a good fit and discuss how my services can meet your needs.

What are your rates for each service?

Individual Counseling: Ksh 2,000 per session (2 hours)
Group Counseling: Ksh 1,000 per person per session (1.5 hours)
Family Therapy: Ksh 5,000 per session (2 hours)
Child Therapy: Ksh 2,500 per session (1 hour)
Unique Case-Based Therapy: Rates vary; please contact for a personalized quote.

What languages do you offer therapy in?

I offer therapy sessions in English

Are virtual sessions available?

Yes, I offer virtual sessions via secure video conferencing platforms for clients who prefer or require remote therapy.
